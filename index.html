About Me

AI Enthusiast | Research-Oriented Developer

I am Asif Al Amin, a Computer Science and Engineering student at Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT), passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Data Analytics.

I enjoy transforming innovative ideas into intelligent systems that solve real-world challenges. My experience includes AI-based applications, smart agriculture systems, computer vision projects, and research-driven development.

I have worked on projects such as Agro Doctor, an AI-powered agricultural management system designed to support precision farming through intelligent recommendations and decision-making. My technical toolkit includes Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-Learn and modern AI frameworks for building scalable and impactful systems. Beyond development, I actively participate in research activities, innovation competitions, workshops, and technology communities to continuously strengthen my problem-solving and analytical skills.

I am particularly interested in combining AI research with practical implementation to create technologies that contribute meaningful impact to society.
